# Customer Consent Checklist

Use this checklist as a manual confirmation guide. Do not store sensitive customer data here.

## Required Consent
- Customer requested the service.
- Customer understands the target event, quantity, ticket type, seating/price constraints, and maximum budget.
- Customer authorizes the operator to perform only the agreed manual actions.
- Customer understands platform account, transfer, cancellation, and refund rules may vary.
- Customer confirms whether delivery is by platform transfer, email, app account, pickup, or another approved channel.

## Prohibited Storage
Do not store:
- passwords,
- payment card numbers,
- CVV codes,
- one-time codes,
- identity documents,
- bank details,
- full customer address records,
- private account recovery details.

## Consent Evidence
Keep only minimal non-sensitive evidence that consent was obtained, such as a timestamp, communication channel, and internal order reference.
