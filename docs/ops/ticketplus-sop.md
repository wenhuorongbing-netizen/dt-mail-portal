# TicketPlus+ Manual SOP

This SOP is intentionally manual for third-party platform steps.

## Pre-payment customer confirmation

Customer must confirm:

1. Passenger name and birthdate are accurate.
2. Ticket is personal and must match ID.
3. 1st–10th day orders usually cover the current month only.
4. 11th day or later orders usually require current month + next month.
5. Issued tickets are not refundable.
6. This service is independent, not official.

## Operator purchase flow

1. Create order in admin panel.
2. Generate customer mailbox.
3. Confirm customer payment.
4. Open TicketPlus+ manually.
5. Use full mailbox address, e.g. `dt202606001@tickets.buffjo.top`.
6. Fill passenger real name and birthdate.
7. Pay manually.
8. Confirm ticket appears.
9. Process subscription/cancellation according to month rule.
10. Screenshot or record subscription status.
11. Send handover message.
12. Mark order as delivered.

## 10th-day rule operational handling

- 1–10: collect 63 EUR + service fee unless platform checkout shows otherwise.
- 11 onward: collect 126 EUR + service fee because current + next month may be required.

Always phrase as “platform rule / actual checkout rules apply”.

## Handover message checklist

- Webmail URL.
- Mailbox username prefix.
- Mailbox password.
- Full email for TicketPlus+ login.
- TicketPlus+ app login steps.
- Reminder to log in before inspection.
- Reminder that ticket is personal and non-transferable.
- Independent-service notice.

## Do not

- Send only a QR screenshot as final delivery.
- Promise refund after issuance.
- Promise deletion of payment method unless actually verified.
- Promise official affiliation.
