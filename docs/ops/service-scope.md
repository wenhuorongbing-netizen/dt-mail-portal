# Service Scope

## Purpose
TicketPlus ops supports human-reviewed ticket purchasing assistance. It does not replace customer authorization, platform terms, payment compliance, or operator judgment.

## In Scope
- Record high-level order progress using approved status labels.
- Track manual operator checklist completion without storing sensitive data.
- Document platform-specific questions that require review before action.
- Preserve a clear boundary between customer consent, purchasing, delivery, and closure.
- Deliver ticket access through Wallet-only instructions by default while keeping operator-owned payment methods and accounts under operator control.

## Out of Scope
- Crawlers, bots, checkout automation, queue automation, or account-registration automation.
- Holding customer passwords, payment credentials, personal documents, or full payment details.
- Taking custody of customer funds without a separately approved policy.
- Assuming one platform's refund, transfer, account, or cancellation rules apply to another platform.
- Giving customers TicketPlus+ account access while an operator-owned payment method, subscription, or renewal risk remains attached.

## Required Human Confirmation
Before any real order work, a human operator must confirm:
- the platform rules for that order,
- customer authorization scope,
- payment method handling,
- delivery method,
- cancellation or subscription obligations,
- payment method exposure risk,
- data retention limits.
